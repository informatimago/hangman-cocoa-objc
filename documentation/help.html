The hangman game

The computer chooses an random word, and displays the first and last letter, and a dot for each letter in between.

The player must guess the word, by entering letters one after the other.

When a letter entered is in the word, and has not already been entered, the positions where this letter appears in the word are revealed.

Otherwise, the computer draws a stick on the hang man diagram.

When the hang man diagram is completed, the player loses.

When all the letters of the word are revealed, the player wins.

Mouse

You may select a letter by clicking on the corresponding button.

You may start a new game by clicking on the "New Game" button.

Keyboard

You may select a letter by typing it on the keyboard.

You may start a new game by typing the "RETURN" or "ENTER" key.
